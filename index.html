About Us

The Palate is a fine dining Indian restaurant located in Sion, Mumbai. Known for delicious food, excellent service and a vibrant atmosphere, it is a perfect destination for family dinners, parties and celebrations.


Our restaurant features spacious seating, private dining rooms for events, a lively sports bar and two levels of cocktail bars serving premium beverages.
